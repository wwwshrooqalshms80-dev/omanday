celebrations in oman

The Sultanate of Oman is distinguished by its celebrations, which reflect the depth of its history and the pride of its people in their authentic customs and traditions. During national occasions, the streets are decorated with flags, and military and artistic shows are held to highlight the spirit of unity and belonging. The sky lights up at night with fireworks displays that create an atmosphere of joy and excitement, and additional fireworks shows are also held across the different wilayats. Military parades are also organized as part of the celebrations. Families celebrate their heritage through traditional chants and dances such as Al-Razha and Al-Aazi. During religious holidays, a sense of joy and family closeness prevails, as traditional Omani dishes are prepared and people visit one another.
